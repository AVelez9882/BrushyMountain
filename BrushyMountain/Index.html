Welcome to the

End of the Line.

Known as the "End of the Line", Brushy Mountain State Penitentiary was the last place you wanted to be sent to. Brushy was a maximum-security prison that held murderers, robbers, rapists, and evil men who committed heartless crimes with little remorse. Today, it's open to the public to explore it's history.
